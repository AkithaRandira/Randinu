Who Are We

 
History of DriveTribe

Drive Tribe is a unique car club that was founded in 1985 with a vision to promote the education and understanding of new automotive technologies, particularly in the areas of autonomous driving and vehicle safety. The club is dedicated to providing students and car enthusiasts with a platform to learn about these cutting-edge technologies, through organized events, workshops, and online resources. With a focus on education and collaboration, Drive Tribe has become a hub for individuals who are passionate about cars and eager to stay abreast of the latest advancements in the field. Whether you are a student pursuing a career in automotive engineering or simply a car lover with a keen interest in the latest automotive innovations, Drive Tribe is the perfect community for you.

Drive Tribe is that was established by a group of dedicated automotive enthusiasts. Over the years, the club has grown into a thriving community of car lovers who share a common passion for driving, performance, and style. Drive Tribe is committed to providing a platform for like-minded individuals to come together and share their love for cars, whether it be through organized events, social gatherings, or online discussions. With a focus on camaraderie, education, and fun, Drive Tribe has become one of the most respected car clubs in the industry, attracting members from all over the world who share a common love for all things automotive.

Automotive technologies

Drive Tribe is committed to providing its members with the latest information and resources on cutting-edge automotive technology. Whether you're interested in autonomous driving, electric vehicles, or the latest in vehicle safety features, the club's website is your go-to source for all things automotive. From informative articles and guides to instructional videos and podcasts, Drive Tribe's resources cover a wide range of topics, making it easy for members to stay up-to-date on the latest developments in the industry. Additionally, the club hosts regular workshops and seminars led by experts in the field, providing members with hands-on experience and the opportunity to learn directly from industry professionals. Whether you're a student looking to pursue a career in automotive technology or simply a car enthusiast eager to stay ahead of the curve, Drive Tribe has the resources and information you need to stay informed and engaged.

In addition to its online resources, Drive Tribe also hosts in-person events and activities that allow members to get hands-on experience with the latest automotive technologies. From test-driving electric vehicles to attending conferences on autonomous driving, the club provides numerous opportunities for members to learn, engage, and network with like-minded individuals. Whether you're a student looking to enter the automotive industry or a seasoned professional seeking to expand your knowledge, Drive Tribe is the ideal community for anyone who wants to stay at the forefront of the industry's latest developments.
